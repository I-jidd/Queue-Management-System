Frequently Asked Questions

What is the deadline for adding/dropping?

The deadline for adding or dropping subjects for the current semester is November 20, 2025. No requests will be processed after this date.

How do I get my transcript of records?

To get your transcript, you must:

  1. Download the "Transcript Request Form" from the section above.
  2. Pay the corresponding fee at the Cashier's Office.
  3. Get a "Get a Batch Number" from this website and select "Submit Transcript Request."
  4. Bring your receipt and the filled-out form during your time slot.
